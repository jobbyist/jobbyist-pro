FAQ

Before you subscribe

What exactly is Jobbyist Pro?+
Jobbyist Pro is a monthly career management subscription for South African jobseekers and working professionals. It bundles CV and profile tools, recruiter visibility, progress tracking, and ongoing support into one R99/month plan — designed to keep your search moving, not just give you a one-time fix.
What does the 50%-off offer actually include?+
For a limited time, new subscribers pay R49.50/month for their first 3 months (50% off the standard R99/month), then move to the regular R99/month rate. The offer also includes full, free access to The 90-Day Job Search Sprint for as long as you remain a Pro subscriber.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Jobbyist Pro has no long-term contract. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account, or by emailing support@jobbyist.co.za, and you won't be billed for the next cycle.
Is my CV and personal data shared with anyone?+
Your profile is made visible to recruiters searching Jobbyist's database so they can find and contact you — that visibility is the point. Beyond that, your personal data is never sold or shared with third parties outside the normal operation of the platform.
What payment methods do you accept?+
Subscriptions are processed securely through PayPal, which supports most major debit and credit cards in addition to PayPal balances — you don't need an existing PayPal account to pay by card.
Does Jobbyist Pro guarantee I'll get a job?+
No. Jobbyist Pro is a career management and placement-support system, not a recruitment agency — it doesn't place candidates or guarantee employment outcomes. What it does guarantee is a structured, well-supported, AI-enhanced search, including the performance benchmarks published for The 90-Day Job Search Sprint.
